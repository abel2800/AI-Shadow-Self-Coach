## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Ari a replacement for therapy?**
A: No. Ari is a coaching tool designed to support self-exploration and reflection. For serious mental health concerns, please seek professional help.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We use minimal personal information and follow strict privacy guidelines.

**Q: Can I use Ari offline?**
A: No, Ari requires an internet connection to function. However, you can view your journal entries offline.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Check the app store for current pricing. We offer free trials and various subscription options.

### Session Questions

**Q: How long should sessions be?**
A: It depends on the session type:
- Check-in: 3-5 minutes
- Gentle Deep: 15-30 minutes
- Micro Practice: 5-10 minutes

**Q: Can I pause a session?**
A: Yes! You can pause and resume sessions. Your conversation is saved automatically.

**Q: What if I don't know what to say?**
A: That's okay! Start with how you're feeling right now. Ari will guide the conversation with gentle questions.

**Q: Can I delete a session?**
A: Yes, you can delete sessions from your journal. Tap the entry and select "Delete."

### Technical Questions

**Q: The app is slow or not responding.**
A: Try:
- Check your internet connection
- Close and restart the app
- Update to the latest version
- Contact support if issues persist

**Q: I forgot my password.**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. You'll receive reset instructions via email.

**Q: Can I use Ari on multiple devices?**
A: Yes! Your account syncs across devices. Just log in with the same account.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Journal ‚Üí Select entry ‚Üí Tap "Export" ‚Üí Choose format (PDF or text).
