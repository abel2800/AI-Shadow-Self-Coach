## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Open the app ‚Üí Tap "Get Started" ‚Üí Enter email and password ‚Üí Complete onboarding

**Q: What happens during onboarding?**
A: You'll review privacy settings, set your mood baseline, and choose preferences

**Q: Can I skip onboarding?**
A: No, but it only takes 2-3 minutes and helps personalize your experience

### Using Sessions

**Q: What's the difference between session types?**
A:
- **Check-in** (3-5 min): Quick daily reflection
- **Deep Session** (15-30 min): Longer exploration
- **Micro Practice** (5-10 min): Short mindfulness exercises

**Q: How do I start a session?**
A: Home screen ‚Üí Choose session type ‚Üí Set mood ‚Üí Tap "Start"

**Q: Can I pause a session?**
A: Yes! Tap "Pause" and resume later. Your conversation is saved.

**Q: What if I don't know what to say?**
A: Start with how you're feeling right now. Ari will ask gentle questions to guide you.

### Journal & Export

**Q: How do I view my journal?**
A: Tap "Journal" in bottom navigation ‚Üí See all your sessions

**Q: Can I search my journal?**
A: Yes! Use the search bar at the top to find sessions by keyword or tag

**Q: How do I export a session?**
A: Journal ‚Üí Tap entry ‚Üí Tap "Export" ‚Üí Choose format (PDF or text)

**Q: Can I delete a session?**
A: Yes, from the journal entry detail screen, tap "Delete"

### Mood & Progress

**Q: How do I track my mood?**
A: Use the mood slider on Home screen (1-10) or submit mood scores in Analytics

**Q: Where can I see my progress?**
A: Tap "Analytics" in bottom navigation ‚Üí View mood trends and insights

**Q: How often should I check in?**
A: Daily check-ins are recommended, but do what works for you

### Safety & Support

**Q: What if I'm in crisis?**
A: Tap "Resources" ‚Üí Find crisis hotlines ‚Üí Call or text for immediate help

**Q: Is Ari a replacement for therapy?**
A: No. Ari is a coaching tool. For serious concerns, seek professional help.

**Q: What if Ari detects I'm in danger?**
A: Ari will show safety resources and ask if you're safe. Use crisis hotlines if needed.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is slow or frozen.**
A: Check internet connection ‚Üí Close and restart app ‚Üí Update to latest version

**Q: I can't send messages.**
A: Check internet ‚Üí Verify you're in active session ‚Üí Try restarting app

**Q: My sessions aren't saving.**
A: Check internet ‚Üí Ensure logged in ‚Üí Try ending session again

**Q: I forgot my password.**
A: Login screen ‚Üí Tap "Forgot Password" ‚Üí Check email for reset link
