# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Introduction

AI Shadow-Self Coach ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, password (encrypted)
- **Session Data**: Conversations, journal entries, mood scores, session summaries
- **Profile Information**: Preferences, consent settings, safety contacts
- **Therapist Referral Information**: Preferences, consent for contact (if applicable)

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Usage Data**: Session frequency, feature usage, app interactions
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

### Research Data (With Consent)

If you consent to research participation:
- Anonymized conversational data
- Aggregated usage patterns
- No personally identifiable information (PII) is included

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

- **Service Delivery**: Provide coaching sessions, journaling, and progress tracking
- **Personalization**: Customize your experience based on preferences
- **Safety**: Detect high-risk content and provide crisis resources
- **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

### Research Uses (With Consent)

- **Model Training**: Improve AI responses and safety detection
- **Research Studies**: Contribute to mental health research (fully anonymized)
- **Academic Publications**: Share aggregated findings (no individual data)

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to authorized personnel only
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Research Data**: Anonymized data may be retained for research purposes (with consent)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share data only in these circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**: Trusted vendors who assist in operations (under strict confidentiality)
2. **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
3. **Safety Emergencies**: If we detect imminent risk of harm
4. **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition (with notice)

### Research Data Sharing

- Only anonymized, aggregated data
- No personally identifiable information
- Shared with research partners and academic institutions
- Only with your explicit consent

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

- **View Your Data**: Access your sessions, journal entries, and profile
- **Export Data**: Download your journal entries and session transcripts
- **Delete Account**: Request account deletion at any time
- **Update Preferences**: Modify consent settings and preferences

### Consent Management

- **Research Consent**: Opt-in/opt-out of research participation anytime
- **Data Processing**: Control how your data is processed
- **Marketing**: Opt-out of promotional communications (if applicable)

### Data Portability

- Export your data in standard formats (JSON, PDF, text)
- Transfer your data to other services

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

### Data Transfers

- Data may be processed in the United States or other countries
- We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers
- GDPR compliance for European users

### Regional Rights

- **GDPR (EU)**: Right to access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection
- **CCPA (California)**: Right to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
- **Other Jurisdictions**: We respect local privacy laws

## AI and Machine Learning

### Model Training

- Your conversations may be used to train AI models (with consent)
- All data is anonymized before training
- Models are trained to maintain your privacy

### Safety Classifiers

- Automated risk detection analyzes messages for safety
- High-risk content triggers safety protocols
- No human review of routine conversations
